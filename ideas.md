<idea>
**Design Movement**: Coastal Modernism — inspired by luxury travel magazines and high-end hospitality brands

**Core Principles**:
1. Immersive full-bleed imagery as the primary storytelling medium
2. Asymmetric layouts that break the grid to create visual tension and energy
3. Warm blue-to-ocean-teal palette with sun-kissed orange accents
4. Typography-first hierarchy: large editorial headlines anchor each section

**Color Philosophy**:
- Deep ocean blue (#0B3D91) as authority and trust
- Sky blue (#1E90FF) for interactive elements and CTAs
- Warm amber/orange (#F97316) as the accent of adventure and warmth
- Off-white (#F9FAFB) backgrounds with subtle warm undertones
- Emotional intent: trust + wanderlust + warmth

**Layout Paradigm**:
- Hero: Full-viewport image with left-anchored text overlay and floating search card
- Destinations: Masonry-style grid with oversized cards and hover zoom
- Packages: Horizontal scroll on mobile, 3-column on desktop with staggered heights
- Testimonials: Offset card layout with large quote marks as decorative elements

**Signature Elements**:
1. Diagonal section dividers using clip-path for dynamic flow between sections
2. Floating "pill" badges on destination cards (e.g., "Most Popular", "New")
3. Glassmorphism search bar overlaid on hero image

**Interaction Philosophy**:
- Cards lift with subtle shadow on hover (translateY + shadow)
- Images scale gently (1.05x) on hover revealing more of the scene
- Smooth scroll-triggered entrance animations (fade-up) for each section

**Animation**:
- Hero text: staggered fade-in from bottom (0.3s delay between elements)
- Cards: fade-up on scroll enter (Intersection Observer)
- Search bar: subtle pulse glow on focus
- Button: background color shift + slight scale on hover

**Typography System**:
- Display: "Playfair Display" — editorial, elegant, travel-magazine feel
- Body: "DM Sans" — clean, modern, highly readable
- Hierarchy: 72px hero title → 36px section headers → 18px body
</idea>

- Destinations: Tight 4-column grid on desktop with uniform card heights

- Testimonials: Single-column centered with large avatar and star rating

1. Bold section numbers (01, 02, 03) as decorative typographic anchors
